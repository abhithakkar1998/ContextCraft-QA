Runway AI, Inc. (also known as Runway and RunwayML) is an American company headquartered in New York City that specializes in generative artificial intelligence research and technologies. The company is primarily focused on creating products and models for generating videos, images, and various multimedia content. It is most notable for developing the commercial text-to-video and video generative AI models Gen-1, Gen-2, Gen-3 Alpha and Gen-4.
Runway's tools and AI models have been utilized in films such as Everything Everywhere All At Once, in music videos for artists including A$AP Rocky, Kanye West, Brockhampton, and The Dandy Warhols, and in editing television shows like The Late Show and Top Gear.
